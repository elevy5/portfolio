Project Overview

The SaaS Customer Portal was developed for a rapidly growing B2B software company experiencing exponential customer growth. With over 1,000 enterprise clients, their support team was overwhelmed, and customer satisfaction was declining due to slow response times and limited self-service options.

As Product Manager, I spearheaded the development of a comprehensive customer portal that would serve as a centralized hub for account management, support resources, and service requests. The platform now handles 10,000+ daily interactions across 500+ active enterprise clients.

The solution features intelligent automation, personalized user experiences, and seamless integration with existing business systems, resulting in significant improvements in both operational efficiency and customer satisfaction metrics.
