Project Overview

The Project Management Suite was developed to address critical challenges facing modern distributed teams. Our client, a rapidly growing technology company with 200+ employees across 5 countries, was struggling with project coordination, resource allocation, and cross-team communication.

As Product Manager, I led a team of 15 professionals through the complete product lifecycle, from initial concept to successful launch and ongoing optimization. The platform now serves over 500 active users managing 100+ concurrent projects.

The solution integrates project planning, resource management, team collaboration, and performance analytics into a cohesive platform that adapts to various project management methodologies including Agile, Scrum, and Kanban.
